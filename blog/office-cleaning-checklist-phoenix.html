Office Cleaning Checklist: What Should Actually Be Cleaned

Published May 18, 2026 — CleanWorks Solutions

If you've ever wondered "did they actually clean the bathroom today?" this checklist is for you. Here's what a professional commercial cleaning service should cover — and how often.

Daily Cleaning Tasks

  • Empty all trash and recycling bins, replace liners
  • Vacuum all carpeted areas and entrance mats
  • Mop all hard floors with disinfectant
  • Wipe and sanitize all high-touch surfaces (door handles, light switches, elevator buttons, handrails)
  • Clean and sanitize all restrooms (toilets, urinals, sinks, counters, mirrors)
  • Restock soap, paper towels, and toilet paper
  • Clean kitchen/breakroom counters, sink, and microwave exterior
  • Spot-clean glass doors and windows for fingerprints
  • Tidy reception area — straighten chairs, magazines, etc.

Weekly Cleaning Tasks

  • Dust all surfaces including desks, shelves, window sills, picture frames
  • Clean interior windows and glass partitions
  • Deep clean kitchen: inside microwave, refrigerator exterior, cabinet fronts
  • Disinfect phones, keyboards, and office equipment (with approved electronics cleaner)
  • Scrub and disinfect restroom floors and grout
  • Vacuum upholstered furniture
  • Clean baseboards and corners
  • Wipe down vending machine surfaces

Monthly Cleaning Tasks

  • High-dusting: ceiling fans, light fixtures, vents, air returns, exposed ductwork
  • Deep carpet extraction in high-traffic zones
  • Strip and re-wax hard floors (if applicable)
  • Clean behind and under furniture and appliances
  • Wash exterior windows (ground floor)
  • Detail-clean conference rooms: chairs, tables, A/V equipment
  • Deep clean grout lines in restrooms and breakrooms
  • Sanitize garbage bins and recycling containers
  • Wipe down walls and doors for scuffs and marks

Use This Checklist

Print it. Give it to your current cleaner. Ask: "Which of these do you do, and how often?" If their answer doesn't match — or if they get defensive — that tells you everything.
